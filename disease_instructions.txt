For flu:
- Get plenty of rest.
- Drink fluids, such as water, broth, or sports drinks.
- Avoid close contact with others, especially in crowded places.
- Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when coughing or sneezing, then throw the tissue away and wash your hands.
- Take over-the-counter flu medications, such as acetaminophen or ibuprofen, to relieve symptoms.
